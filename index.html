OUR STORY

Where Legacy Meets the Next Generation

Every great journey begins with a place that holds a story.

Long before Aventrix Realty was established, the very place where our office stands today was home to TP Builders, founded by Late Mr. Perumal in 1965. From this very location, he built homes, created lasting relationships, and established a reputation that continues to inspire generations.

The legacy did not end with one generation. In 2010, Gnanasekaran P proudly carried forward the vision of his father, Late Mr. Perumal. Inspired by the legacy of TP Builders, he preserved the values that earned generations of trust while embracing a modern approach to real estate—where tradition, professionalism, and long-term relationships remain at the heart of every property journey.

As the vision continued to grow, Sanjay Gandhi L became part of this new chapter, bringing over a decade of real estate experience across land transactions, residential and commercial properties, joint ventures, and strategic advisory. Together, Gnanasekaran P and Sanjay Gandhi L continue to lead Aventrix Realty, combining a proud legacy with modern expertise to deliver exceptional real estate solutions for homeowners, investors, and developers.

Today, Aventrix Realty represents the next chapter of that journey. Built on decades of local knowledge and guided by a modern outlook, we provide professional advisory services in residential, commercial, land, and joint venture real estate across Chennai.

Honouring the past. Building the future.
